# find.software KickOff at UFZ

On Dec 04th 2025, we kicked of our project in Leipzig at
[UFZ](https://www.ufz.de). Nothing crazy happend, we mainly talked about
project's organisation and management. We also touched some of the first tasks
we will be working on starting in January 2026. In March 2026, we will present
our project at the [deRSE26](https://events.hifis.net/event/2945/) in Stuttgart.
