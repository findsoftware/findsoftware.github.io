We'll be participating at this years [deRSE collaboration workshop](https://events.hifis.net/event/3249/) in Göttingen. The call for participation deadline is reached and here is what we proposed as a joint effort with other research software infrastructure projects:

- [Metadata and Metadata Extraction for Research Software](https://github.com/DE-RSE/2026_collaboration_workshop_de/issues/21)
- [Building a network of research software infrastructure projects](https://github.com/DE-RSE/2026_collaboration_workshop_de/issues/19)
- [Hackathon for the research software infrastructure website](https://github.com/DE-RSE/2026_collaboration_workshop_de/issues/20)
- [Awesome RSE 2026 - Revise and Update awesome RSE](https://github.com/DE-RSE/2026_collaboration_workshop_de/issues/24)

We're happy if you'd join or spread the word.
